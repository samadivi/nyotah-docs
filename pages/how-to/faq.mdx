# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What is Nyotah Cloud?

Nyotah Cloud is a cloud computing platform that offers virtualization services such as virtual machines, networks, and storage resources. It allows users to deploy, manage, and scale their applications and services in a flexible and cost-effective manner.

### How do I sign up for Nyotah Cloud?

To sign up for Nyotah Cloud, simply visit our website at [www.nyotahcloud.com](https://www.nyotahcloud.com) and click on the "Sign Up" button. Follow the prompts to create your account and start using our services.

### Is there a free trial available?

Yes, Nyotah Cloud offers a free trial period for new users. During this trial period, you can explore our platform and services without any cost. Once the trial period expires, you can choose a pricing plan that best suits your needs.

## Billing and Pricing

### How does billing work on Nyotah Cloud?

Nyotah Cloud operates on a pay-as-you-go pricing model. You are billed only for the resources you use, with no upfront fees or long-term commitments. Detailed billing information is available in your account dashboard.

### What payment methods are accepted?

We accept major credit cards and PayPal for payment. You can securely add and manage your payment methods in your account settings.

### Can I change my pricing plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your pricing plan at any time from your account settings. Changes to your pricing plan will take effect immediately, and you will be billed accordingly.

## Technical Support

### Do you offer technical support?

Yes, Nyotah Cloud provides technical support to our users. If you encounter any issues or have questions about our platform or services, you can contact our support team for assistance. Support is available via email, live chat, and phone.

### What kind of support is included with the free trial?

During the free trial period, you have access to our basic support services. This includes assistance with account setup, platform navigation, and general inquiries. For technical issues or advanced support, you may consider upgrading to a paid plan.

### Is there a knowledge base or documentation available?

Yes, we provide comprehensive documentation and tutorials to help you get started with Nyotah Cloud and troubleshoot common issues. You can access our documentation at [docs.nyotahcloud.com](https://docs.nyotahcloud.com) or visit our community forum for additional resources.
