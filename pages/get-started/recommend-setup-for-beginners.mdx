# Beginner Setup Recommendations

Welcome to Nyotah Cloud! If you're new to cloud computing or Nyotah Cloud specifically, this guide will provide you with some recommendations for setting up your environment to get started smoothly.

## 1. Account Setup

Before you begin, make sure you have created a Nyotah Cloud account. Follow the steps outlined in our [Getting Started Guide](getting-started.md) if you haven't done so already. Once your account is set up, log in to the Nyotah Cloud dashboard.

## 2. Understanding the Dashboard

Take some time to familiarize yourself with the Nyotah Cloud dashboard. The dashboard is your central hub for managing your virtual resources. Explore the different sections, such as VM management, networking, and settings, to understand what options are available to you.

## 3. Deploying Your First Virtual Machine (VM)

To get hands-on experience with Nyotah Cloud, deploy your first virtual machine. Follow the steps outlined in our [Getting Started Guide](getting-started.md) to deploy a VM. Choose a simple operating system like Ubuntu or CentOS for your first VM to keep things straightforward.

## 4. Connecting to Your VM

Once your VM is deployed, connect to it using SSH or remote desktop software. Follow the instructions provided in the [Getting Started Guide](getting-started.md) to find the connection details for your VM and establish a connection.

## 5. Exploring Basic VM Management

Once connected to your VM, explore basic VM management tasks such as starting, stopping, and restarting the VM. You can also try installing software packages, configuring network settings, and creating user accounts to familiarize yourself with managing a virtual environment.

## 6. Learning Resources

As you continue to explore Nyotah Cloud, take advantage of the learning resources available to you. Our documentation, tutorials, and community forums are valuable sources of information and support. Don't hesitate to reach out to our support team if you have any questions or need assistance.

## 7. Experimenting Safely

Finally, don't be afraid to experiment! Cloud computing offers a flexible and scalable environment for testing and development. Feel free to try out different configurations, deploy multiple VMs, and explore advanced features as you become more comfortable with Nyotah Cloud.

By following these setup recommendations, you'll be well on your way to mastering Nyotah Cloud and harnessing the power of cloud computing for your projects and applications.
